## 2Ô∏è‚É£ Client Booking System (YOUR CLIENTS' Business)

**File:** `CLIENT_BOOKING_WORKFLOW.md`

**What it is:** The PRODUCT your clients use after they're onboarded

**Players:**
- **End Customers:** People booking with your clients (homeowners, salon clients, etc.)
- **Your Clients:** Business owners using CoreSentia (tradies, salons, etc.)
- **AI Receptionist:** Bot handling bookings for your client
- **Client Dashboard:** Where your clients manage their bookings

**Flow:**
1. End customer contacts your client (SMS/web)
2. AI qualifies and suggests booking times
3. Customer agrees ‚Üí pending booking created
4. Your client gets SMS notification
5. Client confirms ‚Üí both parties get confirmation SMS

**Key Point:** This is the actual service your clients pay for.
